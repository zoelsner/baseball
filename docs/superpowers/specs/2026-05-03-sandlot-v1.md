# Sandlot V1

## Problem Statement

Fantasy baseball tools expose a lot of data, but they do not clearly answer the daily operating question: what should I do before lineup lock, and why should I trust it?

## V1 Promise

Sandlot is a recommend-only fantasy baseball assistant. It turns Fantrax league context into a small set of lineup, waiver, roster, trade, and league decisions.

## Primary User Job

Before lock, help me avoid bad starts and capture obvious point gains without manually checking Fantrax, player news, matchups, projections, and my league context.

## V1 Tabs

- **Today**: matchup state, lock countdown, do-now lineup decisions, watch items, and reviewed/no-action context.
- **Roster**: quiet roster list plus high-level strengths and weaknesses.
- **Adds**: curated waiver targets with suggested drop context and tradeoffs.
- **Trade**: paste or inspect a proposed trade; return take/reject/counter guidance with fairness, weekly impact, age/dynasty impact, and rationale.
- **League**: standings and early league context. Later this becomes trade-partner intelligence.
- **Skipper**: context-aware AI assistant that can answer from full roster, league, matchup, trade, and waiver context.

## V1 Non-Goals

- No automatic Fantrax lineup moves.
- No drops, claims, trade accepts, or irreversible transactions.
- No generic player search as the primary Adds experience.
- No Skipper answer that depends on uncited current news unless the synced/researched context provides it.

## First AI Evals

### Lineup Swap

Scenario: Wendell Park is in an OF slot but is not in the MLB lineup against an LHP. Hideo Tamura is on the bench and confirmed leadoff vs RHP.

Expected behavior: recommend Park out, Tamura in.

Pass criteria:
- Names both players correctly.
- Gets swap direction correct.
- Mentions Park lineup risk.
- Mentions Tamura confirmation or matchup.
- Quantifies projected gain when available.
- Does not recommend dropping Park unless explicitly asked.

### Idle Pitcher

Scenario: Luca Boscarino is in an SP slot but is not pitching today. Pavel Krenz is on the bench and probable.

Expected behavior: recommend Krenz into the SP slot.

Pass criteria:
- Identifies Boscarino as an invalid/low-value daily start.
- Identifies Krenz as the replacement.
- Mentions probable-start status.
- Separates lineup swap from roster transaction.
- Flags data freshness if probable status is stale.

### Trade Grade

Scenario: User gives Tobias Reyna and receives Niko Castellanos plus Rell Brookings.

Expected behavior: grade the trade using weekly impact, roster construction, age/dynasty value, and positional scarcity.

Pass criteria:
- States take/reject/counter clearly.
- Explains weekly projection delta.
- Explains dynasty/age impact.
- Explains roster fit.
- Identifies at least one risk.
- Does not overstate certainty.
